About Better QOLHub

Better QOLHub was created to address a critical need in the Skyblock community: a centralized, trustworthy source for quality-of-life improvements. Too often, players struggle to find reliable mods and clients, or worse, encounter malicious software disguised as helpful tools.

Our mission is simple: provide a curated collection of verified, open-source solutions that genuinely improve your gameplay experience. Every item in our catalog has been thoroughly vetted by our team and comes from reputable developers with established track records.

We believe in transparency at every level. From open-source code to clear documentation about what each tool does, we ensure you have all the information needed to make informed decisions about your Skyblock setup.
